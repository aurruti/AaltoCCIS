The "drugspider" datasets represent the nets made by spiders intoxicated by some drug from experiments made in the 1960s.

"hiv.adj" represent the possible infection routes among the first infected people in the HIV outbreak in America in the 1980s.

"kindergarten.adj" shows friendships among kids in a British kindergarten.

The "picasso" data sets represent the networks of two of Pablo Picasso's "constellation" drawings from 1924.
